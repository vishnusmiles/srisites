History

Slater Tex was established in the year 1970 from humble beginnings to gradually grow to be a leader in Export Markets for Textile Home Furnishing. With our innovative ideas and principles brought newness in Design, Style and uncompromising Quality at a highly competitive price. We created a niche market and established the company as a pioneer destination for a wide range of Home Furnishings including cotton-based home furnishings in Yarn Dyed / Solid Dyed / Printed categories.
