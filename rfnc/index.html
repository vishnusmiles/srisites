Services

Check out the great services we offer

EEG

The patients who presents with fits, Jerks, Up rolling of eyes, sudden unprovoked fall, head injury, sudden transient loss of awareness, sensorium, RTA (Road traffic accident) with fits (or) loss of consciousness.

NCS

This is for the patients who presenting with burning feet, numbness of hands & feet, tingling sensation, unsteadiness while walking which worsens during dark.Pins and needle sensation of limbs, facial deviation, accidents, wrist drop and foot drop.

Evoked potentials

For patients with vertigo dizziness hard of hearing, progressive vision loss, blindness, demyelination, memory disturbance, headache with visual problems, spinal cord problems.

RNS

For drooping of eyelids with diplopia (or) double vision, speech disturbances, swallowing problem, limb weakness with easy fatiguability.

ENMG

Needle is introduced into muscle to study the muscle and nerve problems

TCD

For stroke, (one side limb weakness) to study the blood flow of the brain. 12 cranial arteries are studied. Carotid artery stenosis can be detected. Risk of stroke can be analysed.
